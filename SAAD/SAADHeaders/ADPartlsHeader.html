ADPartls:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Compute the measurement partials
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   ADPartls( el, jDEpoch, jD, spinRate, cant, dihedral, rA, dec )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   el           (6)       Orbital elements
   jDEpoch                The Julian date corresponding to the mean anomaly
                          in el
   jD           (n)       Julian dates at which to compute the data
   spinRate               Spin rate (rad/sec)
   cant         (2)       Cant angles (rad)
   dihedral     (2)       Dihedral angles (rad)
   rA                     Right Ascension (rad)
   dec                    Declination (rad)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   none

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
