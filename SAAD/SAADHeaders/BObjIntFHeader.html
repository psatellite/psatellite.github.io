BObjIntF:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Determines if a bright object will interfere with a sensor scan 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   k = BObjIntF( u, uBO, rhoBO, cant, fOV )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   u                      Spin axis unit vector or [rA,dec];
   uBO          (3,n)     Unit vectors of bright object(s)
   rhoBO        (n)       Angular radii of bright object(s)
   cant                   Cant angle of scanning sensor wrt the spin axis
   fOV                    Field of view of the sensor

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   k                      Indexes of bright objects that interfere

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Children:

Common: Graphics/NewFig
