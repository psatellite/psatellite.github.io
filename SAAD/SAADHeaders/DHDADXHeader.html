DHDADX:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Computes the derivatives of the dihedral angle with respect
   to right ascension and declination
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   H = DHDADX( ra, dec, us, ue )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   ra                     Right Ascension
   dec                    Declination
   us           (3,n)     Unit sun vectors
   ue           (3,n)     Unit nadir vectors

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   H            (n,2)     [èda/èra èda/èdec]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Common: Graphics/NewFig
