DHArcDX:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Computes the Jacobian for an arc measurement. Used for sun angle 
   measurements.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   H = DHArcDX( ra, dec, u )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   ra                     Right ascension
   dec                    Declination
   u                      Unit vector

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   H                      [èh/dra èh/ddec]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
