HDA:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Computes the dihedral angle for a spinning spacecraft.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   da = HDA( uA, uS, uE )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   uA                     Spacecraft unit vector
   uS           (3,n)     Unit sun vectors
   uE           (3,n)     Unit nadir vectors

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   da           (n)       Dihedral angle

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
