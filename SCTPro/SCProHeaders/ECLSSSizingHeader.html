ECLSSSizing:

Path: SCPro/MannedVehicle

% Sizes an Environmental Control and Life Support subsystem (ECLSS).
   Has a demo comparing the three types.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p = ECLSSSizing( nCrew, nDays, type )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   nCrew            (1,:) Number of crew
   nDays            (1,:) Number of days
   type             (1,:) 'open', 'closed', 'partially closed'

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p                (1,1) Data structure
                          .PR    (1,:) Power required (kW)
                          .WH    (1,:) Waste heat generated (kW)
                          .SCM   (1,:) Mass of spare parts and consumables (kg)
                          .SCV   (1,:) Volume of spare parts and consumables (m^3)
                          .HWM   (1,:) Mass of system hardware (kg)
                          .HWV   (1,:) Volume of system hardware( m^3)
                          .TOTSM (1,:) Total system mass (kg)
                          .TOTSV (1,:) Total system volume (m^3)
                          .PI    (1,:) Power impact penalty (kg)
                          .HI    (1,:) Waste heat impact penalty (kg)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	 Reference: Miller, S., "Environmental Control and Life Support System"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
