EclipseDurationSunSync:

Path: SCPro/ProEphemeris

% Dawn-Dusk SSO eclipse calculations
 The Sun-Sync orbit is unique in that the node precesses at a regular rate
 throughout the year. To analyze the eclipses we therefore need a loop
 that includes dates for the year and increments the node. 

   Since version 11.

 See also: SunSyncInclination, SunSyncNode, EarthEclipseDuration
