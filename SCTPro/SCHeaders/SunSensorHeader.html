SunSensor:

Path: SC/Sensor

% Two detector sun sensor.
 The two detectors are at an angle of alpha1 and alpha2 with respect to
 vertical. You get t1 and t2 from running CalibrateSunSensor.

 The detector 1 measurement is assumed of the form:

 f1 = t1(1) + t1(2)*cos(angle+alpha1)+ t1(3)*cos(angle+alpha1)^2

 Type SunSensor for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   angleMeas = SunSensor( t1, t2, alpha1, alpha2, f1, f2, angle )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   t1          (1,3) Detector 1 coefficients [constant linear square]
   t2          (1,3) Detector 2 coefficients [constant linear square]
   alpha1      (1,1) Offset angle sensor 1 (rad)
   alpha2      (1,1) Offset angle sensor 2 (rad)
   f1          (1,:) Detector 1 measurements
   f2          (1,:) Detector 2 measurements
   angle       (1,:) Angle of measurements from the normal (rad)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   angleMeas   (1,:) Measured sun angle (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
