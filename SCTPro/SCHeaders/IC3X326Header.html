IC3X326:

Path: SC/Attitude

% Convert 3x3 inertia matrix to [Ixx Iyy Izz Ixy Ixz Iyz].
   The initial 3x3 form is

   [Ixx Ixy Ixz
    Ixy Iyy Iyz
    Ixz Iyz Izz]

   and the final 1x6 form is

   [Ixx Iyy Izz Ixy Ixz Iyz]

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   y = IC3X326( x )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x           (3,3)     Inertia matrix

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   y           (1,6)     [Ixx Iyy Izz Ixy Ixz Iyz]
