TwoC2F:

Path: SC/GOESRead

% Converts two's complement of length 2b to a floating point number.

   Since version 3.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   f = TwoC2F( hi, lo, b )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   hi              (1,:)   High bits
   lo              (1,:)   Low bits
   b               (1,1)   Bits per word

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   f               (1,:)    Floating point

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
