TRF:

Path: SC/Disturbances

%   Computes an RF torque for a set of antenna.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   t = TRF( u, r, p )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   u                  (3,n or 1) Antenna unit vectors
   r                  (3,n or 1) Moment arms (m)
   p                  (1,1)      Power (watts)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   t                  (3,1)      Torque (Nm)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
