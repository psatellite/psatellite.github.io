% Computes local solar time.
 Compute local solar time from Julian date and inertial position.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   hRA = LocalSolarTime( jD, rECI )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   -------
   Inputs
   -------
   jD   (1,:) Julian date
   rECI (3,:) ECI position vector

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   hRA 	(1,1) Local solar time
