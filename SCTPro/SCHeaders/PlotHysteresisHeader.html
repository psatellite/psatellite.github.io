PlotHysteresis:

Path: SC/Actuator

% Create hysteresis plots.

   Minor loops are dotted. H may have the same number of rows as B
   or have only one row.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   PlotHysteresis( B, H )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   B        (m,n)   B Field 1 major loop, m-1 minor loops
   H        (m,n)   H Field 1 major loop, m-1 minor loops

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Children:

Common: Graphics/NewFig
Common: Graphics/XLabelS
Common: Graphics/YLabelS
