SumRXF:

Path: SC/Attitude

% Sum a set of torques produced by summing T = rxF

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   t = SumRXF( r, f, cM )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   r             (3,n) Position vectors
   f             (3,n) Force vectors
   cM            (3,1) Center of mass

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   t             (3,1) Total torque

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
