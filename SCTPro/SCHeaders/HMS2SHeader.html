HMS2S:

Path: SC/Ephem

% Converts hours, minutes and seconds into seconds

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   s = HMS2S( h, m, s )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   h           (1,:) Hours
   m           (1,:) Minutes
   s           (1,:) Seconds

   or

   h           (3,:) [Hours; Minutes; Seconds]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   s           (1,:) Seconds

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
