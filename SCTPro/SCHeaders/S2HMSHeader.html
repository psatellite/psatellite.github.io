S2HMS:

Path: SC/Ephem

% Converts seconds into hours, minutes and seconds

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [h, m, s] = S2HMS( s )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   s              (1,:) Seconds

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   h              (1,:)  hours
   m              (1,:)  minutes
   s              (1,:)  seconds

   or
   
   h              (3,:)  [hours;minutes;seconds]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
