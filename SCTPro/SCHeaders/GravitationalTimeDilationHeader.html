GravitationalTimeDilation:

Path: SC/Sensor

% Gravitational time dilation.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   tR = GravitationalTimeDilation( mu, r )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   mu             (1,1) Gravitational constant (m^3/s^2)
   r              (1,:) Position of observer

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   tR             (1,1) Ratio of proper time at r to proper time at infinity

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gravitational_time_dilation
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
