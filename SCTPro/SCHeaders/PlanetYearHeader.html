PlanetYear:

Path: SC/Ephem

% Length of year of planets in terms of Earth years.
 Uses Planets for the semi-major axes, which uses the Astronomical Almanac.

 Type PlanetYear for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   y = PlanetYear( k )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   k       (1,:) Planet id

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   y       (1,1) Fraction of Earth year

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
