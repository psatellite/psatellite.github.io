MSolDay:

Path: SC/Ephem

% Computes a mean solar day.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   d = MSolDay( jD )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jD             (1,:)    Julian date (day)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   d              (1,:)    Mean solar day (sidereal days)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References: The Astronomical Almanac for the Year 1993, U.S. Government
               Printing Office, 1993, p. B6. 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
