NavObservables:

Path: SC/Sensor

% Computes optical navigation observables.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
                  NavObservables;
   [c, a, f, m] = NavObservables( r, rP, radiusP, jD, nameP, nameM, alb )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   r                 (3,:)   Trajectory (au)
   rP                (3,:,m) Planet trajectories (au)
   radiusP           (1,m)   Radius of the planets
   jD                (1,:)   Julian dates of trajectory points
   nameP             {1,m}   Planet names
   nameM             (1,:)   Mission name for plot titles
   alb               (1,m)   Albedo fractions for planets
   albF              (1,m)   Albedo fraction

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   c                 (m,:)   Chordwidths of planets and sun (rad)
   a                 (1,:)   Minimum planet angular separation (rad)
   f                 (m,:)   Power flux (W/m^2)
   m                 (1,:)   Range to nearest target (au)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
