DipoleFromField:

Path: SC/MagneticControl

% Computes the dipole for a solid magnet. 

   Assumes b is measured at the end in Tesla. 1 Tesla is 10000 gauss.
  
   Since version 2014.1
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   m = DipoleFromField( b, h )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   b         (1,:)   Magnetic field (T)
   h         (1,1)   Length (m)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m         (1,:)   Dipole moment (ATM^2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
