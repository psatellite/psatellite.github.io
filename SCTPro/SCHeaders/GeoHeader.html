Geo:

Path: SC/Environs

% Computes parameters for a geosynchronous orbit.
   Returns the semi-major axis, period, orbital velocity and mean orbit rate.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [n, a, p, v] = Geo()
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   none                  

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   n             (1,1)   Mean orbit rate
   a             (1,1)   Semi-major axis
   p             (1,1)   Period in seconds
   v             (1,1)   Velocity

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
