% Demonstrate the magnetic control functions in batch mode. 
 The first three plots are from the two axis controller that instantaneously 
 controls two axes while minimizing the squared magnitude of the dipole.
 The second provides three axis control over a period of time
 in which the magnetic field changes significantly.
