VelocityTimeDilation:

Path: SC/Sensor

% Velocity time dilation.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   tR = VelocityTimeDilation( mu, r )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   v              (1,:) Velocity of observer with respect to the source (km/s)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   tR             (1,1)  Ratio of proper time at r to proper time at v = 0

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gravitational_time_dilation
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
