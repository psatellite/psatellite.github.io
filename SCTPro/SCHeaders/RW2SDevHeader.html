RW2SDev:

Path: SC/Attitude

% Converts random walk measured over dT to standard deviation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   n = RW2SDev( randomWalk )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   randomWalk       (:)     Random walk (deg/sqrt(hour))
   

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   n                (:)     Standard deviation (rad/sec)
