MagneticDipoleCart:

Path: SC/Environs

% Computes the magnetic field of a dipole along m.
   m is the dipole vector.

   Type MagneticDipoleCart for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   b = MagneticDipoleCart( m, p )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   m           (3,1)   Dipole moment (ATM^2)
   p           (3,:)   Points (m)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   b           (3,:)   Magnetic field (T)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
