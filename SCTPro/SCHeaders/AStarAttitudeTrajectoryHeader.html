AStarAttitudeTrajectory:

Path: SC/Attitude

% Compute a trajectory to avoid stayout zones using AStar

 Type AStarAttitudeTrajectory for a demo
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   qPath = AStarAttitudeTrajectory( qNow, qTarget, uBody, lat, lon, radius, nGrid )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   q         (4,1) Current quaternion
   qTarget   (4,1) Target quaternion
   uBody     (3,1) Body vector to keep out of stayout zones
   lat       (1,:) Latitude of stayout zone
   lon       (1,:) Longitude of stayout zone
   radius    (1,:) Radius of stayout zone

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   qPath    (4,n) Quaternions for path

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
