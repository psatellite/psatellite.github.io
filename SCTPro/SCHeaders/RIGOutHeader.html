   Since version 1.

   Form:
   [angInc, intRateOld] = RIGOut( intRate, intRateOld, n )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   intRate      (n,1)     Integrated rate
   intRateOld   (n,1)     Old integrated rate

   Outputs
   -------
   angInc       (n,1)     Angular increments
   intRateOld   (n,1)     Old integrated rate
