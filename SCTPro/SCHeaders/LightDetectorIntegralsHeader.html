LightDetectorIntegrals:

Path: SC/Sensor

% Normalized stellar-light detector integral given a star type and detector
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   e = LightDetectorIntegrals( starType, detector )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   starType      {}      'B1' ... 'M2'
   detector      (1,:)   'Si' or 'visual'

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   e             (1,1)    Integral

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	References:	Kayton, M. and W. R. Fried, "Avionics Navigation Systems.
               Second Edition." Wiley. pp. 569.
