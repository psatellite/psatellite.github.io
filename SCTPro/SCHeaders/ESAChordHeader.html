ESAChord:

Path: SC/Sensor

% Compute the delta earth chord for a scanning earth sensor.
   Will plot dW versus roll if there are no outputs.
   Will automatically set scans off the earth to zero.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   dW = ESAChord( rho, delta, roll )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   rho        (1,1)  Earth angular width (rad)
   delta      (1,1)  Scan cant angle (must be less than rho) (rad)
   roll       (:)    Roll angle (rad)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   dW         (1,:)  Delta earth chord (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
