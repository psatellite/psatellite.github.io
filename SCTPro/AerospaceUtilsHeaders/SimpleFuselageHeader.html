SimpleFuselage:

Path: AerospaceUtils/ComponentModels

% Get CAD vertices and faces for a simple fuselage.

      5                  4
      ------------------    ^ +z
      |                 \ 3   |               
      |                 /     |          
       -----------------      ---------> +x +y into the paper
      1                 2
 
 The origin is in the middle of the bottom.

 Type SimpleFuselage for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [v, f] = SimpleFuselage( w, lMiddle, lTop, h, zM )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   w           (1,1)  y width
   lBottom     (1,1)  x length at the bottom
   lTop        (1,1)  x length
   h           (1,1)  z height
   zM          (1,1)  Height of middle point

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v           ( 8,3) Vertices
   f           (12,3) Faces

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
