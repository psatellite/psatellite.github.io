% Get CAD vertices and faces for the SHELS Flange component. 
 SHELS is the Shuttle Hitchhiker Ejection Launch System.

 Type SHELSFlange for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [v, f] = SHELSFlange
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   None

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v          (:,3) Vertices
   f          (:,3) Faces
   volFlange  (1,1) Volume of the flange
