FLagrange:

Path: AerospaceUtils/Coord

% Lagrange point RHS.
 RHS for the fraction of the distance from the primary to the secondary
 at which L1, L2 or L3 exist.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   y = FLagrange( x, v, lPoint )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x           (1,1)  Value at which y is zero
   v           (1,1)  Coefficient
   lPoint      (1,1)  Lagrange point, 1, 2 or 3

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   y           (1,1)  Zero

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
