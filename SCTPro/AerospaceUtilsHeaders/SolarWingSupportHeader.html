SolarWingSupport:

Path: AerospaceUtils/ComponentModels

% Get CAD vertices and faces for a triangle strut for a solar wing.
 l is in the +y direction and w in the +x direction.

 Type SolarWingSupport for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [v, f] = SolarWingSupport( w, l, t )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   w           (1,1)  Panel width (m)
   l           (1,1)  Strut length (m)
   t           (1,1)  Strut thickness (m)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v           (:,3) Vertices
   f           (:,3) Faces

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Common: Graphics/DrawVertices
