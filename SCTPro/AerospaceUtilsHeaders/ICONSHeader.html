% Get CAD vertices and faces for the Integrated Communications and Navigation Sensor.

 Type ICONS for a demo.

 This is an example of how to build up models of devices and compute
 their inertias. The sensor consists of two cameras and gimbals.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [v, f, inr] = ICONS( demo )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   demo        (1,1) Anything draws icons

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v           (:,3) Vertices
   f           (:,3) Faces
   inr         (1,1) Inertia data structure

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
