AlphaCentauriSystem:

Path: Missions/AlphaCentauri

% Model of the Alpha-Centauri system.
 Includes stars A, B and a hyopthetical planet around A.

 Type AlphaCentauriSystem for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   r = AlphaCentauriSystem( jD )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jD             (1,:)    Julian date
   
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   r              (9,:) 	  [rA;rB;rP]. rA: position vector of Alpha
                           Centauri A in km. rB: Position vector of Alpha
                           Centauri B in km. rP: Position vector of a
                           hypothetical planet orbiting at 1 AU around
                           Alpha Centauri A, in km. 
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
