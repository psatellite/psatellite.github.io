TT2UT1:

Path: Common/Time

% Converts TT (Terrestrial (dynamic) Time) to UT1 
 Time scale based on the rotation of the earth)
 UT1 = TT - DT where DT is determined from astronomical
 observations.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   jDUT1 = TT2UT1( jDTT )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jDTT      (1,1) Julian date Terrestrial (Dynamic) Time

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   jDUT1     (1,1) Julian date Universal Time 1

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Children:

Common: Time/DeltaT
