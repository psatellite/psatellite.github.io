Wedge:

Path: Common/ComponentModels

% Generate a wedge between x1 and x2.
 x1 and x2 are on the x-axis. Type Wedge for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [v, f] = Wedge( x1, x2, w, t )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x1          (1,1)  Tip position
   x2          (1,1)  Thick end
   w           (1,1)  Width in y direction
   t           (1,1)  Thickness in z
   

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v           (6,3) Vertices
   f           (8,3) Faces

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Common: Graphics/DrawVertices
