MeasNLSpring:

Path: Common/Estimation

% Computes nonlinear spring measurement
 The measurement is the arctangent of x/d.w
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   y = MeasNLSpring(x,d)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x     	(2,1)	State [x;v]
   d     	 (.)  Data structure
                  .w (1,1) Baseline 

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   y       (1,1)	Measurement

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
