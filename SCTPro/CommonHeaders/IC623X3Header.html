IC623X3:

Path: Common/MassProperties

% Convert an inertia matrix from a 1x6 to a 3x3 format.
 The original form  is 

 [Ixx Iyy Izz Ixy Ixz Iyz]

 to 

 [Ixx Ixy Ixz
 Ixy Iyy Iyz
 Ixz Iyz Izz]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   y = IC623X3( x )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x           (1,6)     [Ixx Iyy Izz Ixy Ixz Iyz]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   y           (3,3)     Inertia matrix

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
