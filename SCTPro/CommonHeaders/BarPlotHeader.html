BarPlot:

Path: Common/Graphics

% Creates a bar plot of x.
 x(k) is assumed held constant from k to k+1. Plots on the current axes.

 Type BarPlot for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   BarPlot( x, y )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x              (1,n)    X-value
   y              (1,n-1)  Y-value

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Common: Graphics/Plot2D
