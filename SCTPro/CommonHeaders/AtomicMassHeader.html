AtomicMass:

Path: Common/Materials

% Outputs atomic mass in kg or amu.
 Type AtomicMass for a demo and list of available atoms.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   m = AtomicMass( atom )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   atom          (1,:)  Atom name
   units         (1,:)  'amu' or 'kg'

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m             (1,1)  Mass (kg or AMU)
   
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
