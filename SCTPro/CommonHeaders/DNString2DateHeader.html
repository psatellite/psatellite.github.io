DNString2Date:

Path: Common/Time

% Converts a day number string to a date.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   dateTime = DN2Date( dN, year )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   dn           (1,1)  Day number
   year         (1,1)  Year

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   dateTime     (1,6)  [year month day hour minute seconds]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
