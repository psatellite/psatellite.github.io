Circle:

Path: Common/Graphics

% Compute x y data for a 2-D Circle.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [x,y] = Circle(r,xc,yc,n)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   r                 (1)     radius
   xc                (1)     x- center
   yc                (1)     y- center
   n                 (1)     number of points around circle

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   x                (1,n)    x-data 
   y                (1,n)    y-data 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
