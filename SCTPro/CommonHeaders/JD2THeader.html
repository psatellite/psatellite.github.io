JD2T:

Path: Common/Time

% Converts Julian days to centuries from J2000.0

 Typing JD2T returns the current Julian century.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   T = JD2T( jd )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jd           (1,:) Julian date (days)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   T            (1,:) Julian centuries of 86400s dynamical time from j2000.0

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
