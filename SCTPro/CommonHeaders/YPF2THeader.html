YPF2T:

Path: Common/Time

% Converts years in fractional form, e.g. 1993.5 to T

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   T = YPF2T( y )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   y       (1,1) Year in fractional form

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   T       (1,1) Julian centuries of 86400s dynamical time from j2000.0

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
