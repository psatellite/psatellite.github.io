Date2FY:

Path: Common/Time

% Computes the fractional year from the date.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   fy = Date2FY( datetime )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   datetime   (1,6) [year month day hour minute seconds]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   fy         (1,1) Fractional year
