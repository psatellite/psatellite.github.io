PlotPayloadFraction:

Path: StraightLine/ConstantThrust

% Plot the payload fraction for a range of transfer times
 Plots the payload fraction and payload per unit power for a range of transfer
 times given the specific target distance.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   PlotPayloadFraction( d )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   d        (.)  Straight2D data structure

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   ts      (1,:) Range of times based on ZeroPayloadTime
   lambda  (1,:) Payload fraction (mP/m0)
   alpha   (1,:) Payload/power (kg/kW)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   See also: ComputePayloadFraction
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
