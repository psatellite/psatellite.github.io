TSky:

Path: Link/RF

% Brightness temperature of the sky.

   Limited to frequencies between 2.5 and 60 GHz.

   Type TSky for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   t = TSky( f, e )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   f            (1,n)  Frequency (GHz)
   e            (1,m)  Elevation angle (deg)
                        
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   t            (n,m)  Temperature
                        
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References:	Maral, G. and M. Bousquet. (1998.) Satellite Communications
               Systems. Wiley. pp. 36.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
