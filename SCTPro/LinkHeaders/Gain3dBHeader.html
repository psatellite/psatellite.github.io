Gain3dB:

Path: Link/RF

% Antenna 3 dB gain for a parabolic antenna.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   g = Gain3dB( theta3DB, fIllumination, eff )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   theta3DB      (1,1)   3 dB Angle (deg)
   fIllumination (1,1)   Illumination factor (deg)
   eff           (1,1)   Efficiency
                        
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   g             (:)   Gain (dB)
                        
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	  References:	Maral, G. and M. Bousquet. (1998.) Satellite Communications
               Systems. Wiley. pp. 18
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
