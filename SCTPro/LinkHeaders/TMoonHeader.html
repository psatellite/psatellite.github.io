TMoon:

Path: Link/RF

% Noise temperature of the Moon. Constant at 250 deg-K.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   t = TMoon( f )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   f             (1,:)  Frequency (GHz)
                        
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   t             (1,:)  Moon noise temperature (deg-K)
                        
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References:	Maral, G. and M. Bousquet. (1998.) Satellite Communications
               Systems. Wiley. pp. 37.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
