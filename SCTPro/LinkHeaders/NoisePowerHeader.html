NoisePower:

Path: Link/RF

% Computes the noise power required from bandwidth and noise temperature.
   Has a built-in demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p = NoisePower( t, b )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   t          (1,:)  Noise temperature (deg-K)
   b          (1,:)  Bandwidth (Hz)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p          (1,1)  Noise Power

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: Swenson, G.W. (2000.) "Intragalactically Speaking." Scientific
              American. July 2000. p. 46.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
