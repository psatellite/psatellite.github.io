--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   f = WavelengthToFrequency( lambda, c )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   lambda  (1,:)     Wavelength (m)
   c       (1,1)     Propagation speed (default is speed of light)
                        
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   f       (1,:)     Frequency
