RadarPulseTrain:

Path: Link/Radar

% Generates a pulse train.

   Type RadarPulseTrain for a demo.

   Since version 11.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p = RadarPulseTrain( fGHz, tau, tauP, t )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   fGHz           (1,1)    Frequency (GHz)
   tau            (1,1)    Pulsewidth (sec)
   tauP           (1,1)    Pulsing period
   t              (1,:)    Time vector (sec)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p              (1,:)    Pulse train

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Common: Graphics/Plot2D
