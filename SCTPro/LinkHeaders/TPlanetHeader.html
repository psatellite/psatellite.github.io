TPlanet:

Path: Link/RF

% Noise temperature of a planet.
   Type TPlanet for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   t = TPlanet( f, planet )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   f             (1,:)  Frequency (GHz)
   planet        (1,:)  Planet, either 'earth' (default) or 'mars'
                        
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   t             (1,:)  Planet noise temperature (deg-K)
                        
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	References:	Ho, C., Slobin, S., Sue, M. and E. Njoku, "Mars Background Noise
               Temperatures Received by Spacecraft Antennas," IPN Progress Report
               142-149, May 15, 2002.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
