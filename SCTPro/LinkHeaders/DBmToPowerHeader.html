DBmToPower:

Path: Link/Radar

% Convert from dBm to power.
 dBm is computed from the power in mW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   power = DBmToPower( dBm )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   dBm           (1,:)    dB of mW signal

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   power         (1,:)    Power (W)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Reference: Edde, B., "Radar," Prentice-Hall, pp. 702-703
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
