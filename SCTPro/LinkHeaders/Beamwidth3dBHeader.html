Beamwidth3dB:

Path: Link/RF

% Antenna 3 dB angle.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   theta3dB = BeamWidth3dB( d, f, fIllumination )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   d             (1,1)   Aperture (m)
   frequency     (1,:)   Frequency (GHz)
   fIllumination (1,1)   Illumination factor (deg)
                        
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   theta3dB      (:)     3 dB angle (deg)
                        
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	  References:	Maral, G. and M. Bousquet. (1998.) Satellite Communications
                   Systems. Wiley. pp. 17-18.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
