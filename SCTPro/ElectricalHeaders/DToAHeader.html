DToA:

Path: Electrical/Components

% Converts a digital word to a voltage.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   v = DToA( c, b, vMax, vMin )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   c   	        (:)     Commands
   b             (:)     Maximum counts (8 bit = 256)
   vMax          (:)     Maximum voltage                      
   vMin          (:)     Minimum voltage                      

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v             (:)     Voltage

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
