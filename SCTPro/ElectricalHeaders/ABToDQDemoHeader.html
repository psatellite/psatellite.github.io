% Transformation from the AB frame to DQ frame

 A simple example that converts from AB coordinates to DQ coordinates, and
 plots the time histories of both signals.
 The AB(C) frame is for a 3 phase generator with phases A, B and C.
 The DQ frame is the (D)irect (Q)uadrature frame. This is a 
 two dimensional coordinate frame that contains all of the information
 that is contained in the three phases. Any number of phases can
 be combined into a DQ frame. Besides having 2 current states instead 
 of n the dynamics no longer contain the electrical rotation angle.
