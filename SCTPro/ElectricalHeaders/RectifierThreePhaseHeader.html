   The switch states are [A+;A-;B+;B-;C+;C-]

   Type RectifierThreePhase for a demo.

   Form:
        
   vO = RectifierThreePhase( v, s )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   v          (3,:)  Voltages
   s          (6,:)  Switch states

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   vO         (1,:)  Output voltage
