SpecificPower:

Path: Propulsion/Electric

% Computes the specific power of engine/power plant combinations
 Power plants are fusion and nep, nuclear electric propulsion.
 Engines are DFD (direct thrust production), Lithium force accelerator,
 ion and Hall. The specific powers are combined as:
 
   sigma = 1/(1/sigmaP + 1/sigmaE)

 Type SpecificPower for the specific power for a NEP and Hall
 combination.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [sigma,sigmas] = SpecificPower( powerPlant, engine )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   powerPlant	(1,:)  'fusion', 'solar' or 'nep'
   engine      (1,:)  'dfd', 'lifa', 'ion', 'hall', 'mpd'
 
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   sigma       (1,1)   Specific power (kW/kg)
   sigmas      (1,2)   Specific power of [plant engine]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
