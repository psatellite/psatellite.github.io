MolWt:

Path: Propulsion/Gas

% Computes the molecular weight of a gas mixture.

   Since version 2.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [mM, nM] = MolWt( m, n )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   m             (:)     Molecular weight of the constituents
   n             (:)     Number of moles of each constituent

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mM            (1,1)   Molecular weight of the mixture
   nM            (1,1)   Number of moles in the mixture

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
