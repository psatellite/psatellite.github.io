GasMass:

Path: Propulsion/Gas

% Generates a blowdown curve of mass vs pressure.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [mFuel, density] = GasMass( vTank, mWt, T, p )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   vTank        (1,1)   Tank volume
   mWt          (1,1)   Molecular weight of the fuel (default is xenon)
   T            (1,1)   Temperature (deg-K)
   p            (1,:)   Pressure 

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mFuel        (1,:)   Mass of the fuel
   density      (1,:)   Density of the fuel

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
