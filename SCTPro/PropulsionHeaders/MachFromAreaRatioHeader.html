MachFromAreaRatio:

Path: Propulsion/Gas

% Computes the Mach number from area ratio. The throat is at Mach 1.

   Type MachFromAreaRatio for a demo.

   Since version 8.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   M = MachFromAreaRatio( aR, gamma )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   aR            (1,1)   Area ratio Exit Area/Throat Area
   gamma         (1,1)   Ratio of specific heats

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   M             (1,1)   Mach number

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
