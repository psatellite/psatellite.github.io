RocketEquationRelativistic:

Path: Propulsion/Utilities

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   The rocket equation including special relativity.
   Demo is for a boron-proton rocket.
   
   Since version 10.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   mR = RocketEquationRelativistic( u, iSp )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   u            (1,:) Velocity change  (km/s)
   Isp          (1,1) Specific impulse (sec)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mR           (1,:) Mass ratio final mass/initial mass
