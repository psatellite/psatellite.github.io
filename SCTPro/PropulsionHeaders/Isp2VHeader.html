Isp2V:

Path: Propulsion/Rocket

% Compute specific impulse from exhaust velocity
 Specifying "g" is optional.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   v = Isp2V( iSP, g )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   iSP         (1,:)  Specific impulse (sec)
   g           (1,1)  Acceleration of gravity (9.80665 m/sec^2)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v            (1,:)  Exhaust velocity 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
