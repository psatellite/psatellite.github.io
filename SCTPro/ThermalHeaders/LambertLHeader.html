LambertL:

Path: Thermal/ThermalAnalysis

% Lambert's cosine law.

   Type LambertL for a demo.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   dE = LambertL( theta, E )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   theta	(1,:) Angle (rad)
   E       (1,:) Total emissive power

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   dE      (1,:) Flux per unit solid angle (dE/dw)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References:	Thomas, L. C., Heat Transfer, Prentice-Hall, 1992,
               pp. 271-273. 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
