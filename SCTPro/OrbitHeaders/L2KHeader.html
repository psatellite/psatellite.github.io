L2K:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Converts eccentric longitude to true longitude

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   K = L2K( P1, P2, L )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   P1                   e sin(longitude of pericenter)
   P2                   e cos(longitude of pericenter)
   L                    True longitude

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   K                    Eccentric longitude

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
