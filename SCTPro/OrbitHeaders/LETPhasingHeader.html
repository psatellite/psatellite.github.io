LETPhasing:

Path: Orbit/LowEnergyManeuver

% Calculate the nominal transfer time for a low energy transfer
 The names of the moon and planet must be available in the Constant database.
 Type LETPhasing for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [det,orbits] = LETPhasing(x0,desiredPhasing,moon,planet,et,jDate)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x0            	(1,1) Initial guess for a transfer time
   desiredPhasing	(1,1) Angle of the moon at the final time in the
                         Sun/Planet rotating coordinate system
   moon           	(1,:) String name of moon
   planet         	(1,:) String name of planet
   et            	(1,1) Seconds after the epoch jDate at which the
                         transfer initiates
   jDate           (1,1) Julian date
    
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   det             (1,1) Estimated transfer time
   orbits         	(1,1) Number of lunar orbits during transfer

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
