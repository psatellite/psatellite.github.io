AZLaunch:

Path: Orbit/OrbitMechanics

% Launch azimuth to achieve a desired orbit inclination.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   az = AZLaunch( i, lat )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   i           (1,:)  Desired inclination (deg)
   lat                Latitude (deg)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   az                 Launch azimuth (deg)                 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
    References: Wiesel, W. E., Spaceflight Dynamics, McGraw-Hill, 1988,
                pp. 76-77.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
