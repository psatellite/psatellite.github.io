Nu2MAbs:

Path: Orbit/OrbitMechanics

% Computes the mean anomaly from the true anomaly without wrapping btwn -pi / pi
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   M = Nu2MAbs( e, nu, tol, nMax );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   e                     Eccentricity
   nu                    True anomaly (rad)
   tol                   Tolerance                      (optional)
   nMax                  Maximum number of iterations   (optional)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   M                     Mean anomaly (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
