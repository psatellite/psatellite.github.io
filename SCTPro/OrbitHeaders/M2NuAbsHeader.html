M2NuAbs:

Path: Orbit/OrbitMechanics

% Computes the true anomaly from the mean anomaly without wrapping.
   Between -pi / pi
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   nu  = M2NuAbs( e, M, tol, nMax )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   e                     Eccentricity
   M                     Mean anomaly (rad)
   tol                   Tolerance                      (optional)
   nMax                  Maximum number of iterations   (optional)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   nu                    True anomaly (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
