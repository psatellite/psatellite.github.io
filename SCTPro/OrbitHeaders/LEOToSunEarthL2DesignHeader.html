LEOToSunEarthL2Design:

Path: Orbit/ThreeBody

% Design a low thrust transfer from Earth orbit to Sun/Earth-Moon L2 point 
   Simulate in the CRTBP system. Apply a continuous thrust starting from a
   circular Earth orbit at a specified altitude, creating a spiral arc that
   eventually escapes Earth orbit and heads toward the L2 point. Later apply
   a short insertion thrust arc to put the spacecraft into a stable HALO
   orbit about L2.

   Since version 11.
