RefineXAxisIntercept:

Path: Orbit/LowEnergyManeuver

% Gain a precise X axis intercept with a good initial guess 

 Type RefineXAxisIntercept for a demo

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [tF, yF] = RefineXAxisIntercept(y,t0,tF,tol,mu)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   y              (6,1)    current state vector [r;v]
   t0             (1)      initial time
   tF             (1)      initial estimate of final time
   tol            (1)      convergence tolerance
   mu             (1)      CRTBP mass ratio

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   tF             (1)      Time at the intersection with the x-axis
   yF             (6,1) 	State at the intersection with the x-axis

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
