   Form:
   v = C3ToV( c3, r )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   Inputs
   ------
   c3      (1,:) c3
   r       (1,1) Distance from sun (km)
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v       (1,:) Heliocentric velocity (km)
