C3ToV:

Path: Orbit/OrbitMechanics

% Convert c3 to orbital velocity
 C_3 is a measure of the excess specific energy over that required to just
 barely escape from a massive body. C3 is twice the specific orbital energy
 of the escaping object. Type C3ToV for a heliocentric demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   v = C3ToV( c3, r, mu )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   c3      (1,:) C3 characteristic energy (km^2/s^2)
   r       (1,1) Distance from sun (km)
   mu      (1,1) Gravitational parameter (km^3/sec^2), default sun

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v       (1,:) Heliocentric velocity (km/s)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
