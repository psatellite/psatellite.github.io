TransferAndPhaseAngle:

Path: Orbit/OrbitManeuver

% Computes the transfer and phase angle given transfer time.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [transferAngle, phaseAngle] = TransferAndPhaseAngle( r1, r2, tT, mu )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   r1                    Orbit radius 1
   r2                    Orbit radius 2
   tT                    TransferTime
   mu                    Gravitational parameter

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   transferAngle         Transfer angle (rad)
   phaseAngle            Phase angle (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
 References: Wiesel, W. E., Spaceflight Dynamics, McGraw-Hill, 1988,
               pp. 292.
