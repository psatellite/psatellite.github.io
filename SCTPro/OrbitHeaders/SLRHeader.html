Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Computes the semi-latus rectum.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p = SLR( e, a )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   e  (1,:)  Eccentricity
   a  (1,:)  Semi major axis (or perigee radius for parabola)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p  (1,:)  Semi-latus rectum

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References:   Bates, R.B. Fundamentals of Astrodynamics, pp. 28,34.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
