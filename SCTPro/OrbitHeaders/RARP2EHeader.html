Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Computes the eccentricity from apogee and perigee radii

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   e = RARP2E( rA, rP )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   rA             (1,m)  Apogee radius
   rP             (1,n)  Perigee radius

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   e              (m,n)  eccentricity
