ECEFToECI:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Compute the transformation matrix that rotates ECEF to ECI coordinates.
   Assumes spherical Earth.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   M = ECEFToECI( gMT );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   gMT         (1,1)   Greenwich mean time (radians)
                       

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   M           (3,3)   ECEF to ECI rotation matrix

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
