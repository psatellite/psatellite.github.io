A2Perige:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Computes the perigee radius from a.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   rP = A2Perige( a, e )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   a                     Semi major axis
   e                     Eccentricity

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   rP                    Perigee radius

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References: Bate, R. R., Fundamentals of Astrodynamics. pp. 20-40.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
