TWaitHoh:

Path: Orbit/OrbitMechanics

% Computes the wait time at the target.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   T = TWaitHoh( R1, R2, mu )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   R1                    Orbit radius 1
   R2                    Orbit radius 2
   mu                    Gravitational parameter

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   T                     Wait time (sec)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References: Wiesel, W. E., Spaceflight Dynamics, McGraw-Hill, 1988,
               pp. 294.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
