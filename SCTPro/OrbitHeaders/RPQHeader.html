RPQ:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Computes the orbit position vector in the perifocal plane.

   Since version 1.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   r = RPQ( e, rP, nu )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   e                     Eccentricity
   rP                    Perigee radius
   nu                    True anomaly (rad)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   r                     [xP;xQ;0]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References: Wiesel, W. E., Spaceflight Dynamics, McGraw-Hill, 1988, p. 63.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
