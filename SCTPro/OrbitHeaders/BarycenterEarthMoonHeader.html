BarycenterEarthMoon:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Computes the ECI position of the Earth-Moon Barycenter.
   Uses MoonV1 for ephemeris and Constant to look up the gravitational 
   parameters.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   b = BarycenterEarthMoon( jD )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jD             (1,:)      Julian date (days)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   b              (3,:)      Barycenter
