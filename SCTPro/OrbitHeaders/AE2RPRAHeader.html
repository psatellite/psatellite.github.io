AE2RPRA:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Converts semi-major axis and eccentricity to right perigee and apogee.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [rP, rA] = AE2RPRA( a, e )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   a               (1,:) Semi-major axis
   e               (1,:) Eccentricity

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   rP              (1,:) Perigee
   rA              (1,:) Apogee

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	References: Bate, R.R., et. al. Fundamentals of Astrodynamics, pp. 70-71.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
