RHSLinOrb:

Path: Orbit/RHSOrbit

% Continuous form of linearized orbit (Hills equations).
 x and y are in the orbit plane. x is radial. z is out of plane.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [xDot,A,B] = RHSLinOrb( x, ~, n, u)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x     (6,1) [x;y;z;vX;vY;vZ] [dr;rtheta;z;ddr/dt;drtheta/dt;dz/dt]
   t     (1,1) Time (unused)
   n     (1,1) Orbital Rate (rad/s)
   u     (3,1) Control acceleration

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   xDot  (6,1) d[x;y;z;vX;vY;vZ]/dt
   A     (6,6) State matrix 
   B     (6,3) Input matrix

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
