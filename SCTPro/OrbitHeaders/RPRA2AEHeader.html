RPRA2AE:

Path: Orbit/OrbitCoord

% Converts perigee and apogee to semi-major axis and eccentricity
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [a, e] = RPRA2AE( rP, rA )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   rP              (1,:) Perigee
   rA              (1,1) Apogee

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   a               (1,:) Semi-major axis
   e               (1,:) Eccentricity

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	  References:	  Bate, R.R., et. al. Fundamentals of Astrodynamics,
                 pp. 70-71.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
