AlphaCentauriOpticalLink:

Path: Interplanetary/AlphaCentauri

% Create an optical link budget for an Alpha-Centauri mission.
 Computes a link budget for transmission from an Earth-orbit-based station
 to a receiver in the Alpha Centauri system. Therefore there are no
 atmosphere losses.  
 -------------------------------------------------------------------------
  References: Lambert, S. G. and W. L. Casey (1995.) Laser Communications
              in Space. Artech, Boston.
