CRTBP2kms:

Path: Interplanetary/LowEnergyManeuver

% Scales CRTBP Jacobi coordinates to km and sec
 Scales coordinates from Jacobi coordinates of the Circular Restricted Three
 Body Problem to units of kilometers and seconds
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [stateOut,sec2tu] = CRTBP2kms(stateIn,distanceUnit,muPrimary,muSecondary);
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   stateIn       (6,1)  Initial state vector 
   distanceUnit	(1,1)   Distance between Primary and Secondary bodies (km)
   muPrimary     (1,1)   Gravitational Parameter of Primary body
   muSecondary  	(1,1)   Gravitational Parameter of Secondary body
   
   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   stateOut     	(1,6)  Output state vector
   sec2tu        (1,1)   Scaling factor for time

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
