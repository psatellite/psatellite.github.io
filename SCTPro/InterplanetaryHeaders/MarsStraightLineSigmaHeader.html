% Mars straight line trajectory
 Study the dependency on specific power. What is necessary for a fast Mars
 trip? The model is:

   P    = 1/2 T uE/eta
   mDot = T/uE
   m    = (1+f)mF + mE + mP
   mE   = P/sigma
   mF   = mDot*tau
