AtmDensMars2:

Path: Interplanetary/Envionment

% Computes the atmospheric density of Mars.
   For h > 100 km the temperature is kept constant.

   Type AtmDensMars2 for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [rho, t, p] = AtmDensMars2( h )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   h          (1,:)        Altitude (km)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   rho        (1,:)        Atmospheric Density (kg/m^3)
   t          (1,:)        Temperature (deg-K)
   p          (1,:)        Pressure (N/m^2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: http://www.grc.nasa.gov/WWW/K-12/airplane/atmosmrm.html
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
