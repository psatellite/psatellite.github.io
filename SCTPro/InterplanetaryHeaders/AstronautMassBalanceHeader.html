AstronautMassBalance:

Path: Interplanetary/CrewedMission

% Computes astronaut mass balance for long duration missions.
 Computes the mass of the consumables and astronauts themselves. Water may
 be excluded.

 p is an array of percentiles for the crew mass from 0 to 100. 
 For astronaut mass we average a Japanese woman and American male.
 The results are adjusted for crew composition.

 Type AstronautMassBalance for a demo with p = [0 30 60 100] and 
 mF  [1 0 1 0]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [m,mA] = AstronautMassBalance(nDays,p,mF,recycleWater)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   nDays        (1,1) Number of days 
   p            (1,:) Percentile of crew member
   mF           (1,:) Array of male (1) or female (0)
   recycleWater (1,1) If true remove water from the mass

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m           (1,1) Total mass of consumables (kg)
   mA          (1,1) Total mass of astronauts (kg)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: M. K. Ewert and C. Stromgran, "Astronaut Mass Balance for
              Long Duration Missions," 49th International Conference on
              Environmental Systems ICES-2019-126, 7-11 July 2019, 
              Boston, Massachusetts
