 Can plot multiple sets of data. Type PlotXYTrajectory for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   PlotXYTrajectory( x, y, i, xScale, name )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------

   y       (:,:) Y coordinates (m)

   Outputs
