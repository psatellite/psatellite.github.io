--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   y = GStarUKFNL( x, mfd )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   x   (6,1	Attitude states (sigma points)
   d   (.)	Data structure

                              starMeas: [3xn double]
                              starCat: [4x600 double]
                              starID: [1xn]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   y                 (n,1)    Expected measurement
