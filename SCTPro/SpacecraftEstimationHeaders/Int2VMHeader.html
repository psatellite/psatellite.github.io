Int2VM:

Path: SpacecraftEstimation/StellarCatalog

% Converts intensity in watts/m^2 to apparent visual magnitude.
   The apparent visual magnitude of the Sun is -26.85.

   Since version 3.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   v = Int2VM( i )
------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   i               (:)     Intensity (watts/m^2)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v               (:)     Visual magnitude

------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References:   Berry, M. (1981). Principles of cosmology and 
                 gravitation. Cambridge University Press,
                 Cambridge, England, p. 10.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
