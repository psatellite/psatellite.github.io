LightDef:

Path: SpacecraftEstimation/StellarEphem

% Compute gravitational light deflection.

   Since version 3.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p1 = LightDef( p, e, q )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   p       (3,1) Vector from observing body to observed body
   e       (3,1) Vector from sun
   q       (3,1) Vector to observed body from sun
   

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p1      (3,1) Corrected p

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   References: Siedelmann, P., ed. (1992). Explanatory Supplement to the
               Astronomical Almanac, University Science Books,
               Mill Valley, CA, 136-137. 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
