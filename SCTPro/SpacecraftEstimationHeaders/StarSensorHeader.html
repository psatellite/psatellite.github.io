StarSensor:

Path: SpacecraftEstimation/StellarModels

% Computes the output of a star sensor.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   starData = StarSensor( qECIToB, qBToS, uS, starMatrix, fov, fScale )
------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   qECIToB      (4,1)     Quaternion from body to ECI axes
   qBToS        (4,1)     Quaternion from body to sensor axes
   uS           (3,1)     Unit vector (unused)
   starMatrix   (4,p)     Star matrix [intensity;u]
   fov          (1,1)     Circular field-of-view
   fScale       (1,1)     Sensor scale factor

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   starData     (3,m)     Vector of [intensity;xloc;yloc]

------------------------------------------------------------------------
   See also SqrFOV
------------------------------------------------------------------------
