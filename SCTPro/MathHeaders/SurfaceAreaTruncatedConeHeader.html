SurfaceAreaTruncatedCone:

Path: Math/Geometry

% Compute the surface area of a truncated cone
 Returns the surface area of the cone and does not include the bases
 Type SurfaceAreaTruncatedCone for a demo,
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [a,lS,aB] = SurfaceAreaTruncatedCone(rT,rB,l)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   rT  (1,1) Top radius (m)
   rB  (1,1) Bottom radius  (m)
   h   (1,1) Vertical height from B to T  (m)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   a   (1,1) Cone surface area (m^2)
   lS  (1,1) Slant height (m)
   aB  (1,1) Sum of the bases area (m^2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
