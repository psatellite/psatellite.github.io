Dot:

Path: Math/Linear

% Dot product with support for arrays. 
 The number of columns of w and y can be:

 - Both > 1 and equal
 - One can have one column and the other any number of columns

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   d = Dot ( w, y )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   w                 (:,:)  Vector
   y                 (:,:)  Vector

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   d                 (1,:)   Dot product of w and y

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
