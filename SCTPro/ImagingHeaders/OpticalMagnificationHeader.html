OpticalMagnification:

Path: Imaging/Optics

% Computes optical magnification of a telescope.

   Type OpticalMagnification for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   m = OpticalMagnification( fO, fE )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   fO            (1,1)   Focal length objective
   fE            (1,:)   Focal length eyepiece

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m		      (1,:)   Magnification
