PixelFlux2D:

Path: Imaging/Optics

% Compute the integrated flux from a pixel in 2D.

   Type PixelFlux2D for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   PixelFlux2D( e, q )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   e           (1,1)   Data structure 
                       .lambda (1,1) Wavelength (nm)
                       .f      (1,1) Focal length (mm)
                       .a      (1,1) Aperture (mm)
                       .i0     (1,1) Peak flux
                       .mu     (1,1) Centroid (mm);
   q           (1,n)   Coordinates of pixel edges

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   f           (1,n-1) Integrated flux over a pixel

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
