FocalLengthFromAngleOfView:

Path: Imaging/Optics

% Compute the focal length from angle of view.
 If the imaging element is 2-dimensional it will compute two f.

 Type FocalLengthFromAngleOfView for a demo.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   f = FocalLengthFromAngleOfView( alpha, d )
------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   alpha           (2,1) Angle of view (rad)
   d               (2,1) Dimensions of film or imaging chip

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   f               (1,1) Focal length

------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angle_of_view
