Path: Imaging/Optics

% Laser power.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p = LaserPower( d, lambda, z, power )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   d        (1,1)   Aperture (m)
   lambda   (1,1)   Wavelength (m)
   z        (1,:)   Distance (m)
   power    (1,1)   Input power (W)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p        (1,:)   Power at z (W/m^2)
   r        (1,:)   Beam radius (m)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
