StellarMagnitude:

Path: Imaging/Optics

% Stellar magnitude for a variety of sources.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
               StellarMagnitude
   [i, type] = StellarMagnitude( mIn, units )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   mIn      (1,1) Magnitude
   units    (1,:) 'lux' or 'w/m^2'

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   i        (1,1) Intensity
   type     (1,:) Example of this intensity

   See also VM2Int
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
