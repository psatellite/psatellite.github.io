ElectronsToIons:

Path: Imaging/Optics

% From electrons compute the number density of the ions.

   Typing ElectronsToIons gives the electrons in a D-He3 reaction.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   nI = ElectronsToIons( nE, zI )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   nI        (1,2)  Number densities of the electrons
   zI        (1,2)  Electrons per ion

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   nI         (1,1) Number of electrons   

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
