AiryDiskInPixels:

Path: Imaging/Optics

% Compute the pixel width of the Airy disk.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   p = AiryDiskInPixels( f, lambda, a, w )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   f           (1,1)   Focal length (mm)
   lambda      (1,1)   Wavelength (nm)
   a           (1,1)   Aperture (mm)
   w           (1,1)   Pixel width (micro-m)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   p           (1,1)   Airy disk radius (pixels)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Common: Graphics/Plot2D
