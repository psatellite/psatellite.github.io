MinTOrbitInjection:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Constant thrust minimum time orbit injection.
   Compute the initial and final burn angles and total delta-v for a
   constant thrust minimum time trajectory. Assumes a constant gravity
   field and a flat planet model. Assumes a constant mass vehicle.

   Since version 1.0
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [dV, beta] = MinTOrbitInjection( h, u, a, g )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   h           (1,1) Altitude
   u           (1,1) Final horizontal velocity
   a           (1,1) Acceleration
   g           (1,1) Gravitational acceleration.

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   dV          (1,1) Total delta-v
   beta        (2,1) [beta0;betaF] (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   See also FMinTOrbitInjection
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: Bryson, A. E. and Y. Ho, "Applied Optimal Control', 
              Hemisphere, pp. 84-86.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
