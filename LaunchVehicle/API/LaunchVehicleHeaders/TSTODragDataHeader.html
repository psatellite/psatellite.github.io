TSTODragData:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Generates TSTO drag data for use byLiftAndDragJonesSearsHaack.
   Assumes a horizontally launched ferry stage with an orbiter stage.

   Type TSTODragData for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   d = TSTODragData( dO, dF )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   dO       (.)  Orbiter data structure
                  .massH2      (1,1) Mass of liquid hydrogen
                  .massO2      (1,1) Mass of liquid oxygen
                  .wingArea    (1,1) Wing area
   dF       (.)  Ferry data structure 
                  .massH2      (1,1) Mass of liquid hydrogen
                  .massO2      (1,1) Mass of liquid oxygen
                  .massJP      (1,1) Mass of jet fuel
                  .wingArea    (1,1) Wing area

   ------
   Outputs
   -------
   d        (.)  Data structure
                 .l	(1,1) Aircraft length (m)
                 .h	(1,:) Altitude (m)
                 .p	 (.)  Standard Atmosphere structure
                 .b 	(1,1) Span (m)
                 .s	(1,1) Surface area (m^2)
                 .v	(1,1) Volume (m^3)
                 .sL (1,1) Lifting surface  (m^2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Children:
