ProbToLLR:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Convert probabilities to log likelihood ratios.

   Type ProbToLLR for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   lLR = ProbToLLR( p )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   p         (1,:) Probability

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   lLR       (1,:) Log likelihood ratio

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: Blackman, S. and R. Popoli, "Design and Analysis of  Modern
              Tracking Systems," Artech House, 1999.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
