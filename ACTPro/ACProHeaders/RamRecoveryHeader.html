RamRecovery:

Path: ACPro/ACEngine

% Ram recovery in an inlet. 
   This computes the loss of pressure due to shocks. It is based on
   Military Specification 5008B.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   etaR = RamRecovery( machNo )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   machNo     (1,:)   Mach number

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   etaR       (1,:)   Ram recovery

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
