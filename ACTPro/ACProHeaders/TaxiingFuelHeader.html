TaxiingFuel:

Path: ACPro/ACPerformance

% Compute taxiing fuel.

   Based on A320 and B777 data.
   Type TaxiingFuel for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   mF = TaxiingFuel( t, m )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   t             (1,1)    Taxiing duration (min)
   m             (1,:)    Liftoff mass (kg)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mF            (1,:)    Taxiing fuel (kg);
