% Hypersonic coefficient of pressure.

 Type CPHypersonic for a demo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   cP = CPHypersonic( m, gamma )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   m       (1,:) Freestream Mach number
   gamma   (1,1) Ratio of specific heats (Default = 1.4)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   cP      (1,:) Pressure coefficient
