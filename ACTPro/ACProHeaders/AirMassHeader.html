AirMass:

Path: ACPro/Atmosphere

% Computes the relative air mass to sea level and a vertical path.
   Uses the standard atmosphere.

   Type AirMass for a demo
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   m = AirMass( h0, theta )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   h0             (1,1)  Altitude (km)
   theta          (1,:)  Angle from vertical (deg)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m              (1,:)  Relative air mass

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
