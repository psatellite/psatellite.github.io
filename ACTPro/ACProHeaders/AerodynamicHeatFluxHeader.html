Path: ACPro/ACThermal

% Heat flux as a function of mach number.

   Type AerodynamicHeatFlux for a demo.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   qDot = AerodynamicHeatFlux( m, l, p )
------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   m               (1,:)     Mach number
   l               (1,1)     Characteristic length (m)
   p                (.)      Standard atmosphere

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   qDot            (1,:)  Heat flux (W/m^2)
