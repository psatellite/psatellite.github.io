StaticPumping:

Path: ACPro/ACEngine

% Static pumping of air.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [mDot, v] = StaticPumping( cP, altitude, pR, g )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   cP         (1,1)   Specific heat
   altitude   (1,1)   Altitude (m) (Unused if g entered)
   pR         (1,:)   Pressure ratio
   g          (1,1)   Standard atmosphere

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mDot       (1,:)   Mass (kg/m^2)
   v          (1,:)   Velocity

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
