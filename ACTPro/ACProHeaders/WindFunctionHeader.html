WindFunction:

Path: ACPro/Wind

% Approximate the Horizontal Wind Model (HWM93) with analytic functions
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [mag,dir] = WindFunction( alt, lat, day, check );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   alt        (1,M)    Altitude (km)
   lat        (1,N)    Latitude (deg)
   day         (1)     Day of the year
   check       (1)     Flag, indicates whether to check against HWM93

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mag        (M,N)    Wind magnitude (m/s)
   dir        (M,N)    Wind direction, measured north to east (deg)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
