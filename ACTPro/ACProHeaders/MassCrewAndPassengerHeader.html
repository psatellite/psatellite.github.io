MassCrewAndPassenger:

Path: ACPro/ACPerformance

% Mass of crew and passengers.
   Includes all associated mass that is dependent on the number of 
   passengers. It estimates the number of flight attendants.

   Type MassCrewAndPassenger for a demo.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   m = MassCrewAndPassenger( nFlightDeck, nPassenger, longRange )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   nFlightDeck (1,1) Number of flight deck members
   nPassengers (1,1) Number of passengers
   longRange   (1,1) Any entry will give numbers for long range aircraft

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m           (1,1) Mass (kg)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: http://adg.stanford.edu/aa241/structures/
              componentweight.html
