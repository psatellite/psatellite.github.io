Path: Airships/Mass

% Estimate the precent rate of change of mass per minute across altitude
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   mDot = AirshipMDot( h, hDot );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   h          (1,:)    Array of altitudes (m)
   hDot        (1)     Nominal climb rate (m/s)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mDot       (1,:)    Percent rate of change of mass

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
