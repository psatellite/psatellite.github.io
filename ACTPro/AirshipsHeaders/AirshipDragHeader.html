AirshipDrag:

Path: Airships/Modeling

% Compute the approximate drag on an airship.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   D = AirshipDrag( h, u, A, Cd );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   h           (1)       Altitude           [m]
   u           (1)       Velocity           [m/s]
   A           (1)       Reference Aera     [m^2]
   Cd          (1)       Zero-incidence drag coeffcient

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   D           (1)       Total drag force   [N]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
