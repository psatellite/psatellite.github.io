GasProperties:

Path: Common/Materials

% Returns gas properties.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [m, gamma] = GasProperties( gas )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   gas           (1,:)  Name of gas to be used. One of:
                        'hydrogen', 'helium', 'nitrogen', 'air',
                        'argon', 'krypton'

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m             (1,1)  Molecular weight
   gamma         (1,1)  Ratio of specific heats

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
