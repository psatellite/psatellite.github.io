DMS2Rad:

Path: Common/Transform

% Convert degrees, minutes and seconds to radians.
   Inputs can be d,m,s, [d;m;s] or d:m:s or d:m or d.

   Type DMS2Rad for a demo

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   rad = DMS2Rad( d, m, s )
   rad = DMS2Rad( [d;m;s] )
   rad = DMS2Rad( d ); 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   d           (1,:)     Degrees or [d;m;s] or string
   m           (1,:)     Minutes
   s           (1,:)     Seconds

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   rad         (1,:)     Angle in radians

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
