AnimateVectors:

Path: Common/Graphics

% Animate one or more time-varying vectors.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   fig = AnimateVectors( v1, color1, v2, color2 )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   v          (3,n)    Time-varying vector
   color      (1,n)    Color of vector, i.e. 'r' or [1 0 0]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   fig         (1)   	Handle to figure.
