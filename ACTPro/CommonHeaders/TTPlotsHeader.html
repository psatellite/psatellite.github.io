TTPlots:

Path: Common/Control

% Generates torque transmission plots.

   Inputs are magnitude vectors and frequency vectors.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   TTPlots( mag, w, t, gU, title )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   mag            (n,m)  Magnitude vectors
   w              (1,m)  Frequency vector
   t              (n,p)  Titles (strings)
   gU                    Gain units (string)
   title                 Page title

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   None                  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
