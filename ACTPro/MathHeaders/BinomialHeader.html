Path: Math/Analysis

% The binomial expansion of (x + y)^n
 The coefficients are for [x^n x^(n-1) ... x^0]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   a = Binomial( y, n )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   y                  Argument
   n                  Power

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   a                  Coefficients [n n-1 n-2 ... 0]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
 	References:	Spiegel, M. R., Mathematical Handbook, McGraw-Hill,
               1968, p. 3.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
