Path: Math/Linear

% Vector cross product for multicolum arrays. 
 The number of columns of w and y can be:

 - Both > 1 and equal
 - One can have one column and the other any number of columns

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   wxy = Cross( w, y )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   w                 (3)    Vector
   y                 (3)    Vector

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   wxy               (3)    Vector cross product of w and y

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
