WrapPhase:

Path: Math/Trigonometry

% Wrap a phase angle (or vector of angles) to keep it between -pi and +pi
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   angle = WrapPhase( angle )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   angle            (1,:)    Phase angle [rad]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   angle            (1,:)    Wrapped phase angle between -pi and +pi [rad]
