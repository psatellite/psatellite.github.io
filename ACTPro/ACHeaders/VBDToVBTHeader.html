VBDToVBT:

Path: AC/ACCoord

% Compute the total velocity derivative from the body velocity 
   and its derivatives.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   vTDot = VBDToVBT( vB, vBDot )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   vB          (3,1) Velocity in the body frame
   vBDot       (3,1) Acceleration in the body frame

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   vTDot       (1,1) Acceleration

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
