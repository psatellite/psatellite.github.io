BatchRSim:

% Batch Reactor Simulation
 Simulates a batch reactor. Exothemic reaction carried out in a perfectly
 mixed batch reactor with a constant temperature cooling coil.
 Second-order irreversbile reaction.

  Since version 1.
 -------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Reference: Henson, M. A. and D. E. Seborg. (1997.) Nonlinear 
             Process Control, Prentice-Hall. pp. 312-313.
 -------------------------------------------------------------------------
  See also:  RHSBatchR, TimeGUI, Plot2D
