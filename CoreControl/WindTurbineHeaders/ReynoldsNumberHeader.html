ReynoldsNumber:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reynolds number.

   Default nu is for air at sea level at 20 deg-C.

   Type

   ReynoldsNumber

   for a demo

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   rE = ReynoldsNumber( u, l, nu )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   u          (1,:) Velocity (m/s)
   l          (1,1) Length (m)
   nu         (1,1) Kinematic viscosity (m^2/s)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   rE         (1,:) Reynolds Number 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Reference: http://www.windpower.org/en/stat/unitsw.htm#visco
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
