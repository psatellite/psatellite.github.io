VAWTDemoBldMdlRHS:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   VAWT blade model with a pitch controller.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   xDot = VAWTBldMdlRHS( t, x, flag, d )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   t                  Time       (1,1) Not used
   x                  States     (2 + 2*(number of blades),1)
   d                  Structure containing model parameters
                      .nBlades   (1,1) Number of blades
                      .VWind     (1,1) Wind velocity (m/s)
                      .R         (1,1) Rotor radius (m)
                      .alprefmag )1,1)
                      .A         (1,1) Blade area (m^2)
                      .Jb        (1,1) Blade inertia (kg-m^2)
                      .rho       (1,1) Atmospheric density (kg/m^3)
                      .Omdes     (1,1) Desired angular rate (rad/s)
                      .phi       (1,1) Phase angle (rad/s)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   xDot               States     (2 + 2*(number of blades),1)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
