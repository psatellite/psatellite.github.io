PowCompAnlytBsd:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Constant wind model that returns power and angles of attack.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [Pow, alpOut] = PowCompAnlytBsd( t, x, d )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   t            (1,1) Not used
   x                  states: (2+2*(number of blades),1)
   d                  Data structure containing model parameters
                      .nBlades (1,1) Number of blades
                      .R       (1,1) Radius (m)
                      .vWind   (1,1) Velocity of wind (m/s)
                      .rho     (1,1) Density (kg/m^3)
                      .A       (1,1) Blade area (m^2)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   Pow                (1,1)         Power
   alpVEC             (d.nBlades,1) Angles of attack vector

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
