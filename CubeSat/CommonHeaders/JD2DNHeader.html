JD2DN:

Path: Common/Time

% Compute the day number from Julian date.

 Typing JD2DN returns the current day number.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   dayNumber = JD2DN( jD )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jd            (1,1) Julian date

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   dayNumber     (1,1) Day number
   year          (1,1) Year

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
