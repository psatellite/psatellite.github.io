--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   beta0 = EllipePhase( alpha0 )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   alpha0	(1,1)	Desired phase angle at equator crossing (rad)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
