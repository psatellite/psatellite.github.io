JD2SS1970:

Path: FormationFlying/Utility

% Converts a Julian Date to seconds since 00:00:00 GMT, Jan. 1, 1970.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   s = JD2UTC( jD );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   jD         Julian date

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   s          Seconds since 00:00:00 GMT, Jan. 1, 1970.
