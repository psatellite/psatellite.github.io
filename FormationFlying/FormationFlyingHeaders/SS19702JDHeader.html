SS19702JD:

Path: FormationFlying/Utility

% Converts seconds since 00:00:00 GMT, Jan. 1, 1970 to a Julian date.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   jD = SS19702JD( s );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   s          Seconds since 00:00:00 GMT, Jan. 1, 1970

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   jD         Julian date

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
