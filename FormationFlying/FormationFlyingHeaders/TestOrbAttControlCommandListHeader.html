TestOrbAttControlCommandList:

Path: FormationFlying/CommandLists

% Command list for the testing the controller. Two spacecraft initialized
   in a leader-follower formation, plus a small semi-major axis difference.
   One is initialized as the reference. The other is commanded to achieve 
   non-centered out-of-plane periodic motion. Ideal actuation and perfect
   state-sensing are assumed. No gravitational perturbations are modeled.
   The eccentricity may be set in the TestControlSimStruct file.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   cmd = TestControlCommandList;
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   none

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   cmd          (.)    Data structure of commands to be sent to the cluster
