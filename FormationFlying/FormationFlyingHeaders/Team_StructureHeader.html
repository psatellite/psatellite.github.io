Team_Structure:

Path: FormationFlying/DataStructures

% Initialize a team data structure.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   team = Team_Structure( n );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   n            (1)    Number of team structures to generate

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   team         (.)    Team data structure with fields:
                          - teamID
                          - nMembers
                          - memID
                          - refID
                          - captainID
