GeometryEcc2Circ:

Path: FormationFlying/Transformation

% Convert an eccentric geometry structure to a circular geometry structure. 

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Usage:
   gCirc = GeometryEcc2Circ( w, gEcc );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   w             (1)   Argument of perigee [rad]
   gEcc          (.)   Geometric goals data structure, for eccentric orbits

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   gCirc         (.)   Geometric goals data structure, for circular orbits
   
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
