DeltaAlfriend2El:

Path: FormationFlying/Transformation

% Compute standard differential elements from Alfriend differential elements.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Usage:
   [dEl,T] = DeltaEl2Alfriend( e, w, M, dElA );
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   e           (1)  Eccentricity
   w           (1)  Argument of perigee [rad]
   M           (1)  Mean anomaly [rad]
   dElA       (1,6) Orbital element differences [a,th,i,q1,q2,W]

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   dEl        (1,6) Orbital element differences [a,i,W,w,e,M]
   T          (6,6) Transformation matrix
   
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
