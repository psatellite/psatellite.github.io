% Calculates the probability of collision given two ellipsoids using volume relations.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Form: 
  [P] = CollProbVol( P1,xc1,P2,xc2,volSC )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Inputs: 
  -------
  P1, P2     (3,3)  Ellipsoid definitions
  xc1, xc2   (3,1)  Centers of ellipsoids (m)
  volSC      (1)    Volume of object in question   

  Outputs:
  --------
  P          (1)    Probability of collision
