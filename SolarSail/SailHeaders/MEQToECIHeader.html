MEQToECI:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Transform to ECI frame from tangential coordinates.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   m = MEQToECI( r, v )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   r          (3,1)  Position (km)
   v          (3,1)  Velocity (km/s)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   m          (3,3) Transformation matrix

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
