AccelToSailProps:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Sail characteristic acceleration to mass or area for a perfect sail.
   Will create a matrix if two vectors are input. Otherwise it will output
   a row vector.
   Has a built-in demo for a range of acceleration and mass.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   area = AccelToSailProps( accel, mass )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   accel         (1,m)  Sail acceleration (m/s2)
   mass          (1,n)  Spacecraft total mass (kg)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   area     (1,n) or (m,n) Sail area, m2

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
