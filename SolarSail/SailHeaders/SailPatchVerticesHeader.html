SailPatchVertices:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Sail vertices for a default sail in the Y/Z plane
   Sail is a diamond with length 0.5 along booms

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   v = SailPatchVertices
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   v         (3,3)        Vertices for a sail patch

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
