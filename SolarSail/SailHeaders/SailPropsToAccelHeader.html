SailPropsToAccel:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Sail characteristic acceleration from mass and area.
   Will create a matrix if two vectors are input. Otherwise it will output
   a row vector. Has a built-in demo showing the impact of sail sizing.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   acc0 = SailPropsToAccel( area, mass )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   area          (1,m)  Sail area (m2)
   mass          (1,n)  Sail mass (kg)

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   acc0     (1,n) or (m,n) Characteristic acceleration, m/s2

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
