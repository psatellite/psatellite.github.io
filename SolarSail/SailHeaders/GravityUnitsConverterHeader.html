GravityUnitsConverter:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Converts mu from km^3/sec^2
   Possible output units are au^3/sec^2, au^3/day^2,
   m^3/sec^3 or m^3/day^2.

   Type GravityUnitsConverter for a demo.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   mu = GravityUnitsConverter( mu, dist, time )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   mu            (1,1)    Gravitational constant in km^3/sec^2
   dist          (1,:)    Either 'au' or 'm'
   time          (1,1)    Either 'days' or 'sec'

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   mu            (1,1)    Gravitational constant in units^3/sec^2

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
