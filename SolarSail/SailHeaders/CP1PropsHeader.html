CP1Props:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Front and back optical and thermal properties of CP1 (Lambertian)
    2.2 um CP1 with aluminized front and optically clear back.

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [optical, infrared, thermal] = CP1Props
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   none

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   optical         (:)   Optical properties
   infrared        (:)   Infrared properties
   thermal         (:)   Thermal properties

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
