ConeClockConvention:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Apply a selected cone/clock sign convention for angle ranges.
   1: Cone angle limited to [0,pi/2]
   2: Clock angle limited to [0,pi)

   Since version 7.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Form:
   [cone, clock] = ConeClockConvention( cone, clock, iConv )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

   ------
   Inputs
   ------
   cone           (1,:)  In-plane angle (rad)
   clock          (1,:)  Out-of-plane angle (rad)
   iConv          (1,1)  Index of desired convention

   -------
   Outputs
   -------
   cone           (1,:)  In-plane angle (rad)
   clock          (1,:)  Out-of-plane angle (rad)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
